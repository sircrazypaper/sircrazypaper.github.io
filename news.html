An update to the rules about judging

spacer

On The 8th of July 2023 a new section called "Personal judging" has been added to the rules. It provides the option that competitors can time their own inspection, and some people like that better. We hope that this will make some peoples results better. Happy cubing!

A small update to the rules

spacer

On the 3rd of July we updated the formats section of our rules to make it easier to understand how the different formats work. We also fixed a typo and updated the procedure section to make it more compatible with the head to head format. We hope that this will help people understand the rules better.

spacer

On June 10 we finally released the webite for IN-TURN-ER CUBING! We have spent a long time developing it and it is finally here. There are many functions already and we arent even done developing the website yet! If you have any ideas for extra features or updates feel free to email us at jk.lehmann@icloud.com. We hope that you will attend many of our competitions and have lots of fun cubing with us. See you later.
