San Francisco

San Francisco is a beautiful city, it’s a mixture between city and nature. You can go take a walk on the beach or in the beautiful urban areas, look at the many seals on Pier 39, and much more. San Francisco also has a TON of tasty food options to satisfy anyone's hunger, and hotel prices that you will LOVE, in short San Francisco is THE place to go on your vacation. We have much more useful information about the golden city.

Travel options

If you want to get around the city, the most popular way is taking the cable cars. Taking these cable cars are a fun way to get around the city. The cable cars are a national landmark in San Francisco and they were invented nearly 150 years ago. A single ride costs 8$ no matter how old you are. You can get tickets by paying with change, through the MuniMobile app or by using a Clipper card.

The Muni metro is a fast way to get around the city, so it’s perfect for those who might be a bit impatient. For payment there are two options, the Clipper card or the MuniMobile app. Both options are very easy, but you can use the clipper card for multiple Bay area transit systems. If you are 18 or under you can get tickets for free on a regular Muni.

If you want a more fun way to get around, bikes and scooters are definitely the right option for you, the bikes are located all around the city. You can get a single ride that lasts 30 minutes for 3.49$ or a day pass for 10$ a day. A day pass is 24 hours of unlimited 30 minute rides on classic bikes. You can also get some that last a month or more, but that won't be necessary if you are just going on a vacation. You can download the app here!

If you want to try the most futuristic option there is, you can get driven around San Francisco in one of Waymos autonomous vehicles. It is a car that can drive without needing a human to steer it, you can book a ride anytime via an app on your phone. It may seem a little scary at first but it is safer than a human driving, the car has had over 40 million miles of real life driving experience and is packed with the latest technology to ensure that your ride will be safe. Waymos cars are definitely an amazing and exciting experience for anyone who wants a ride around town.

Places to stay

Found Union Square by Sonder is a good hotel to choose if you are looking for a family friendly and very affordable hotel. The hotel is located in the heart of San Francisco and has multiple restaurant options nearby. The hotel offers a lounge and work space and is very kid friendly. A good and affordable room for a family is the double bunk bed room, with four twin beds, air condition and one bathroom. The room costs just 731$ for one week. They don’t offer breakfast at the hotel, but there are a lot of good cafes and restaurants nearby. So this hotel is really a good option if you are on a budget or just want to save some money.

A hotel that we would a hundred percent recommend is Hotel Kabuki. Hotel Kabuki is affordable, with fantastic rooms, where you really will feel comfortable. They offer fitness facilities, an indoor garden with a fishpond and much more. The hotel is located in San Francisco's japantown and is connected to the Nihonmachi mall. This hotel is really nicely furnished and has a great cozy vibe going on. A good room for a family is the double room with two double beds, balcony and city view. The room costs 2700$ for one week at the hotel, breakfast included. So if you like cozy hotels go choose this one.

If you want to get the full experience and choose to book a luxury hotel, The Fairmont hotel is an excellent choice! It looks like a palace inside and out, you really feel like a royalty there. The place is filled with chandeliers, fancy furniture and gorgeous rooms. The Fairmont hotel offers fitness facilities, suites, penthouses, a library, body treatments, aromatherapy, facials and massages. A good room to choose if you want something that is family friendly, is the deluxe room with 2 queen beds, city view and tower for 461$ per night breakfast not included. If you are willing to spend a lot of money but in exchange get a really nice room, you should choose the main building balcony suite with a queen bed and a double sofa bed for 894$ per night breakfast not included. People really love this hotel because it’s animal friendly, offers a lot of gorgeous common rooms and views. The fairmont hotel is also spotted really great. The beach is really close, the Pier 39 is a 4 min drive from the hotel and the oracle park is a 3 min drive from the hotel. This is the perfect hotel for you if you like luxury and experiences. Bon voyage!

Things to do

If you are visiting San Francisco a must is to go see the golden gate bridge. It is an iconic landmark and a very beautiful place to see both morning, afternoon and evening. On Thursdays and Sundays you can get a free walking tour, all scheduled tours can be seen here! On the tours the guides will tell you all about how it was made and why it is such an amazing bridge.

Pier 39 is an important and exciting place to see if you are in San Francisco. At Pier 39 you can look at seals, enjoy one of the many lovely food options and look at Alcatraz from afar. If you want a family photo for your grandparents, Pier 39 is the place to take it, the view is fantastic and the seals are really cute.

If you are a sports person San Francisco is home to some of the best sports teams in the states. You can go to the Chase Center, home of the Golden State Warriors basketball team or Oracle Park, home of the San Francisco Giants baseball team, to watch exciting games and enjoy the locals cheering their teams on.

Exploratorium is an amazing museum for all ages, with both indoor and outdoor exhibitions. At Exploratorium you can explore human behavior, optical illusions, living cells and rainbows. Exploratorium also has a nice museum shop where you can buy all sorts of exciting things and the tickets start at just 40$!

If you want to explore the beautiful nature, muir woods is the perfect place to go. Muir woods is a forest full of California's iconic redwood trees, you can get a half day guided tour and trip starting at around 90$. The trip also goes to Sausalito so you can see two things in the same trip which is awesome. You can book the trip here!

Places to eat

San Francisco is a city where the majority of restaurants are seafood restaurants. There is also a big grill bar culture in the city with an average rating 0f 4.3 stars which is the highest rating for the 100 most popular cities in the US. San Francisco is also known for its great Mexican restaurants. You will be able to read about our favorites below, but keep in mind that there are many more awesome restaurants in San Francisco.

Trestle is a seafood restaurant mostly for adults. Trestle is located at st. Jackson street and is and place you should visit if you want the most luxurious food you can get in all of San Francisco.

Frascati is an Italian restaurant that has been serving the best Italian food in San Francisco since 1901, on 23rd street. The restaurant is for all ages and is not that expensive for the quality and service. The place itself is very nice and cozy. Frascati is definitely a place to visit, if you are near the restaurant.

Papito Hayes is Mexican restaurant with good personal experience. We can tell you that this restaurant is a cheap and good Mexican restaurant, with a nice adult and kids menu. Papito Hayes is located on st. Hayes street. If you want a really good burrito, Papito Hayes is the place to go!
